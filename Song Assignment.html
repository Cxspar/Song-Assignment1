About The Song

"Under Cover of Darkness" is the single served as the lead single for their fourth studio album, angles, and was released online on February 9, 2011. The song symbolizes war, and more specifically how the band was basically at war with each other due to stressful tour schedules, creativity, and clashing emotions. The album "Angles" was the first Strokes album to be written by all five band members.

About The Strokes

Formed in 1998 in New York City, The Strokes are an American indie rock band. The lineup includes Julian Casablancas as the primary vocalist and songwriter, alongside guitarists Nick Valensi and Albert Hammond Jr., bassist Nikolai Fraiture, and drummer Fabrizio Moretti. The band played a significant part in the early-2000s post-punk revival and garage rock revival scenes.
